How column math works

➕ Addition — Carrying

Start from the Units column. If two digits add up to 10 or more, write the ones digit in the answer and carry the 1 to the next column to the left.

   ¹
  4 7
+ 3 5
= 8 2

➖ Subtraction — Borrowing

If the top digit is smaller than the bottom, borrow 10 from the next column to the left — reduce that digit by 1 and add 10 to your current column.

  ⁶¹²
  7 2
− 4 7
= 2 5
