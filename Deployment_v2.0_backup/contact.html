❓ Frequently Asked Questions

🎮 How do I access the games?

All our games are available directly on our website without registration. Simply click on any game from the main page to start playing!

💾 Is my child's progress saved?

Yes! Game progress is automatically saved on your device using local storage. No personal information is sent to our servers.

📱 Do the games work on tablets and phones?

Absolutely! Our games are designed to work on computers, tablets, and smartphones with touch controls optimized for each device.

🆓 Are the games really free?

Yes! All our educational games are completely free. We're supported by child-safe advertising that helps us keep the content free for everyone.

👶 What ages are the games suitable for?

Our games are designed for children ages 3-6, covering preschool and kindergarten learning levels. Content is age-appropriate and educational.

🌐 Do I need internet to play?

You need internet to initially load the games, but once loaded, many features work offline. For the best experience, we recommend staying connected.

🏫 Can teachers use these in classrooms?

Yes! Teachers and educators are welcome to use our games in classrooms. Contact us at countrysnews@gmail.com for more information.
