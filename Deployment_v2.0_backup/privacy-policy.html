👶 Children's Privacy

We do NOT knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. Our games are designed to be safe and educational without requiring personal data collection.

What we do:

  • ✅ Provide educational games without requiring registration
  • ✅ Use child-safe advertising through Google AdSense
  • ✅ Comply with COPPA (Children's Online Privacy Protection Act)
  • ✅ Monitor content to ensure it's appropriate for children

What we don't do:

  • ❌ Collect names, addresses, or contact information from children
  • ❌ Ask children to provide personal information to access games
  • ❌ Share children's information with third parties
  • ❌ Use tracking cookies for children under 13

📊 Information We Collect

Automatically Collected Information:

  • Usage Analytics: We use Google Analytics to understand how our games are used (anonymously)
  • Technical Information: Browser type, device type, screen resolution (for optimal game performance)
  • Game Progress: Stored locally on your device only, not sent to our servers

Advertising Information:

  • Ad Serving: Google AdSense may use cookies for age-appropriate advertising
  • Ad Personalization: Can be disabled in your browser settings

🍪 Cookies and Tracking

We use minimal cookies only for:

  • Essential Functions: Game progress saving (local storage)
  • Analytics: Understanding how to improve our games (Google Analytics)
  • Advertising: Displaying appropriate ads (Google AdSense)

Parents can:

  • Disable cookies in browser settings
  • Use incognito/private browsing mode
  • Clear browser data at any time

🔒 Data Security

We implement security measures to protect any information collected:

  • HTTPS Encryption: All data transmission is encrypted
  • No Data Storage: We don't store personal information on our servers
  • Regular Updates: We keep our security measures current
  • Limited Access: Only authorized personnel have access to analytics data

👨‍👩‍👧‍👦 Parental Rights

Parents and guardians have the right to:

  • Review: Request information about data collection (though we collect minimal data)
  • Delete: Request deletion of any information (contact us)
  • Limit: Disable cookies and tracking in browser settings
  • Contact: Reach out with any privacy concerns

🌐 Third-Party Services

We use these trusted third-party services:

  • Google Analytics: For understanding site usage (anonymized data)
  • Google AdSense: For displaying child-appropriate advertisements
  • Google Fonts: For loading website fonts

These services have their own privacy policies and comply with applicable privacy laws.
