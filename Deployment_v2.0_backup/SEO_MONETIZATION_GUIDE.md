## 🔧 **Setup Instructions**

### **1. Google AdSense Setup**
Replace placeholder IDs in the files:

```html
<!-- Replace in index_seo.html -->
data-ad-client="ca-pub-XXXXXXXXXX"  <!-- Your AdSense Publisher ID -->
data-ad-slot="XXXXXXXXXX"          <!-- Your Ad Unit ID -->
```

**Steps:**
1. Apply for Google AdSense account
2. Get approved (typically 1-2 weeks for quality sites)
3. Create ad units in AdSense dashboard
4. Replace placeholder IDs with real ones
5. Upload and test

### **2. Google Analytics Setup**
Replace GA4 ID in the files:

```html
<!-- Replace in index_seo.html -->
<script async src="https://www.googletagmanager.com/gtag/js?id=G-XXXXXXXXXX"></script>
gtag('config', 'G-XXXXXXXXXX');  <!-- Your GA4 Measurement ID -->
```

### **3. Update Domain References**
Replace `your-domain.com` with your actual domain in:
- `index_seo.html` (meta tags, canonical URLs)
- `sitemap.xml` (all URL locations)
- `robots.txt` (sitemap location)
- `about.html` (canonical URL)
- `privacy-policy.html` (canonical URL)
